
Kit - kat Ice Cream


'Indgredients'

-------------------

1.   1 cup whole milk

2.   3/4 cup sugar

3.   2 Tablespoons cocoa powder

4.   1/4 cup malted milk powder

5.   pinch salt

6.   3 egg yolks(optional)

7.   3 ounces (80 grams) milk chocolate 'chopped'

8.   1 cup heavy cream

9.   1 Tablespoon vanilla

10.  3 full size Kit Kat bars
